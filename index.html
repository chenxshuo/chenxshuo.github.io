I am a PhD candidate at LMU Munich and Siemens, supervised by Prof. Volker Tresp, also affiliated with MCML and relAI. Previously, I graduated from the Elite Master of Data Science with distinction at LMU Munich advised by Prof. Volker Tresp. Before joining LMU Munich, I graduated from the School of Information Management at Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU), Guangzhou, China, in 2020 with a major in Information Systems and a minor in Computer Science, advised by Prof. Daifeng Li.

My ultimate reseach goal is to build Responsible and Trustworthy Super Intelligence. Particularly, I have been working on: the Fluid Intelligence of MLLMs [2][3], LLMs/MLLMs’ Robustness [1] and Model Safety & Red-Teaming [4,5,6,7].
