2.What is the output produced from the following statements? (Treat tabs as aligning to every multiple of eight spaces.)

OUTPUT：

\t 为制表符，在数据处理时经常用来区分表格的列与列之间的界限，也常用来规范输出内容提高易读性。

A tab inserts multiple spaces until the total number of characters on the current line so far is a multiple of 8. So for example, in the string "hi\thello\tgoodbye\tbeautiful\thi", the first \t becomes 6 spaces (because "hi" is 2 characters, so it takes 6 more to get to 8), the second \t is 3 spaces (because "hello" is 5 characters wide, so it takes 3 more characters to get to 8), the third is 1 (because "goodbye" is 7 characters), and the fourth \t is 7 spaces (because "beautiful" is 9 characters, so it takes 7 more to get to 16, which is the next multiple of 8). – From PracticeIt
