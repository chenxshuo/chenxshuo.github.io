In this blog, I would like to discuss, how to kill people and hide the body. Murder is a common cases for polices and suspects normally use several common ways to kill the victims and hide the body afterwards to avoid punishments. Different murder methods will lead to different difficulty for investigators to identify the victims, find useful traces and finally get the suspects. This blog will proivde a detailed introduction to common and unusual ways of murders from the criminal perspective.
